Introduction

This very simple straw poll was based on the appendix A of "Key Issues in Information Systems Management", MIS Quarterly June 1996 [BRANCHEAUJANW96], i.e. previewing the key issues in information systems during the next three to five years. The first poll was conducted in 1996 (28 responses). Yet another simple straw poll was conducted last 2001 (40 responses). Each issue was scored between 0 to 9.
