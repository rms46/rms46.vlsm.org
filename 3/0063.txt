IAB -- 27 July 1999 -- IETF-Announce
------------------------------------

Call for PSO candidates 


IETF,

As explained in Fred Baker's recent message, the IAB will appoint two
interim members to the Protocol Council of the ICANN Protocol
Supporting Organization. This is a call for nominations for these
two positions. Nominations (including self-nominations) should
be sent to iab@iab.org by August 6, 1999.

Note: an almost-final version of the PSO memorandum of understanding
is available at
http://www.ietf.org/internet-drafts/draft-ietf-poisson-pso-bl-02.txt

   Brian Carpenter
   IAB Chair
