NomCom -- 6 March 1999 -- IETF-Announce
---------------------------------------

NOMCOM IESG Results 

The IETF Nominations Committee is pleased to announce its slate for
the open IESG positions.  These candidates have been nominated and
confirmed and take their positions at the end of the March IETF
meeting in Minneapolis.  There were many excellent candidates
considered and it was frequently difficult to make the final choice.

For two year terms as Area co-Director except as othewise indicated:

        Scott Bradner - Transport
        Randy Bush - Operations and Management
        Patrik Faltstrom - Applications
        Thomas Narten - Internet
        Erik Nordmark - Internet - 1 year
        Dave Oran - Routing

Thanks,
Donald, 1998-1999 nomcom chair
