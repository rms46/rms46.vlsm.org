New IAB Members Confirmed by ISOC Trustees

The Internet Society Board of Trusteees has concluded its IAB deliberations
and is pleased to confirm the following nominations for service on the IAB:

Yakov Rekhter
Elise Gerich
Jay Allard
Robert Moskowitz
Erik Huizer
Chris Weider

On behalf of the Board of Trustees, I congratulate the new or
returning IAB members and thank the nominating committee chair,
John Curran, members and assorted liaisons for their commitment
to maintaining the high quality of the Internet Architecture Board
and the Internet Engineering Steering Group.

Vint Cerf
President
Internet Society
