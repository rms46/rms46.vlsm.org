IAB -- 20 October 1999
----------------------

IETF Nominees for PSO seats on ICANN Board 

Following the call for nominations issued by the IAB
on September 14, I am pleased to announce the IETF 
nominees for ICANN Board seats, which we have submitted to 
the Council of the ICANN Protocol Supporting Organization. 
That Council will now proceed to choose the final nominees
from those submitted by the various members of the PSO.

The IETF nominees are

   Vint Cerf
   Patrik Faltstrom
   Geoff Huston
